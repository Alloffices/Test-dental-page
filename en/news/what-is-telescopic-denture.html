What is Telescopic denture? 05/23/2017

 

General info about telescopic denture

-This is a type of denture, which has primary cap part and secondary cap surrounded by artificial denture teeth.

-This denture is removable denture

-Telescopic denture can be supported by both natural tooth and implant. Sometimes abutments can be grouped with bar.

-The metal cap can be made from Alloy metal or Gold.

-The artificial teeth can be made from Acrylic or Porcelain or Zirconia.

-The artificial gingiva can be added depending on the patient's wish or when it is needed.

-The denture is kept inside a mouth by the vacuumm formed between primary cap and secondary cap when it slides down

                             

 This is a picture with primary cap secondary crown

 

What is the procedure?

  1. First, we need to extract hopeless teeth, Root canal treatment if needed and scaling if needed

  2. Prep the remaining teeth for primary cap

  3. Take an impression

  4. You go home with a temporary denture

  5. An impression will be send to the lab

  6. In the second visit, the work is ready for a try in

  7. If it fits good, we glue the primary cap with temporary cement for a try in phase. If it does not fit good, the denture has to be sent to the lab for adjustment.

  8. Finally, at the end we glue the primary cap with permanent glue.

 

For whom is it recommended?

-A person with not many teeth left

-If remaning teeth are not stable

-Patient with gum problem(whenever it is needed, a dentist may take out the denture to cure your gum)

-With Bad oral Hygiene

-Looking for the denture that has good stability

-Looking for a long lasting denture

 

Variations in Telescopic denture

-conventional telescopic denture

-When there are not enough teeth with unstable teeth, remaining teeth may be bound together by the Bar.

-Implant supported telescopic denture, Implant supported bar telescopic denture 

 

What are the advantages of the telescopic denture?

-It is extendable

-Even though the patient looses one or more teeth after getting her/his denture, the patient can still wear the same telescopic denture. A dentist will close the missing site with acrylic to fill up the secondary cap

-When the patient may have implantation futher, the primary cap from the extracted tooth can be replaced on to the impland abutment

-It has the simple design

-compared to the conventional clasp denture, there is no clasp or plate that covers palate

-Short clinical time

-Since there is no surgical procedure, you may visit clinic minimum twice, and there is no suffering period from post surgical complication

 

How to take care of the denture?

-For telescopic denture, please take out denture from a mouth daily at night to brush the denture. However brush the denture just with toothbrush without toothpaste.

-Do not forget to get annual or half annual check up for the telescopic denture
