Dr Frank KannmannBusiness Manager

Dr Frank Kannmann DDS, MSc (in Aesthetic and Restorative Dentistry), MSc (in Anvanced Implant Aesthetics) graduated from Semmelweis University in 1980 and immediately started lecturing and doing research in the Department of Anatomy at the MU of Rostock, where he got his MD and PhD. He is also an expert in Embryology, Anatomy and Histology. He established his first dental clinic in Augsburg in 1985.

In his postgraduate studies he qualified in the field of implantology, laser dentistry and aesthetic dentistry. He loves travelling, scuba diving and underwater photography.
