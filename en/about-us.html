About us 

Our team

Our staff is among the best in their area of expertise. To achieve this, we are improving ourselves on a regular basis, attending conferences, workshops and seminars on international levels.

We do understand that a visit at the dentist is not always the most pleasant experience but we believe that a friendly approach can reduce the stress of the procedure.

more

The clinic

Our clinic is situated in a quiet street of Mosonmagyaróvár, Hungary, near the city center and multiple excellent restaurants and hotels. We built the clinic to be friendly as possible, including a massage and a lounge with a pleasant atmosphere.

To reduce the stress of travel for our clients, we provide airport transfer and borrow a car for visiting the region's sights.

more

Services

We provide dental services ranging from dental restoration through dental implants, fillings, dental surgery, periodontal treatment, cosmetic dentistry to oral hygiene and more.

To do so, we use the best possible equipment and state of art procedures such as computer planned dental implants and restorations and 360-degree X-ray machines on site.
