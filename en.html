about

Who are we?

HD-dental is a dental clinic in Hungary offering a wide range of dental services. Our main profile are dental restorations of any kind - quickly and cheaply.

Our competent and experienced staff at HD-dental utilizing the most modern equipment offer high quality dental treatment to all our clients. Individual treatment planning combined with the use of the latest techniques allows for both effectiveness and flexibility.

Choosing the right dentist can save you quite a lot of money. Due to the low operating costs we can provide our services at prices twice or even three times lower than overseas without compromising quality.

Unlike with other health service providers, at HD dental there is no queue, no waiting list. Whenever you need an appointment or any information just let us know. An email suffices, as does a call or a fax.

Where are we?

HD-dental is located in Mosonmagyaróvár, in the westernmost part of Hungary, next to the main Central European airport hub Vienna-Bratislava. We are within easy reach from all over Europe.

why us?

Why choose Hungary for dental treatment?

Private dental offices in Hungary are equipped with state of the art technology owing to the international clients who have been visiting the country for over twenty years.
HD-dental is also one of them.

Hungarian dentists are trained to cover all aspects of general dentistry. Their qualifications are fully acknowledged in the European Union, and in fact many do work internationally.

Is it really that cheap?

Hungary offers a wide range of excellent dental facilities which visitors can take advantage of at a much lower price than in the USA or the UK.

Indeed, enormous savings can be made by choosing to have your dental work done in our clinic. It can cost less than 50% of the same treatment in the USA or the UK. The favourable economic conditions in Hungary enable us to offer our patients high quality treatment at an affordable, often amazingly low price.

This is in a great part due to the lower operating costs and wages whereas the applied materials are the same well known brands used all over the world.
